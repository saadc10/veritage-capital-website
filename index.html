Our Investment Philosophy

We follow a bottom-up, value investing approach, specialising in small-cap companies with limited analyst coverage. This gives us a competitive edge, allowing us to identify potential mispricings and invest with a margin of safety.

We maintain a concentrated, long-only portfolio with no single position exceeding 20% of our total holdings. Each investment is based on rigorous fundamental analysis, focusing on favourable risk/reward profiles that preserve and grow our capital.

Our sectors of focus include:

  • Information Technology
  • Communication Services
  • Consumer Goods
  • Healthcare
  • Financials
  • Materials & Industrials
  • Energy & Utilities
  • Real Estate
