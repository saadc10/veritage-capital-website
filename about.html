About Us

Veritage Capital is a student-run, simulated investment fund dedicated to providing aspiring finance professionals with hands-on experience in managing a portfolio. Our mission is to foster a deep understanding of investment principles, risk management, and the dynamics of financial markets through active participation in a simulated trading environment.

Our Approach

At Veritage Capital, we believe in a collaborative and research-driven approach to investing. Our team of dedicated students, representing diverse academic backgrounds, actively researches and analyses potential investment opportunities across various asset classes. We utilize a combination of fundamental and technical analysis, coupled with insights from industry experts and mentors, to make informed investment decisions.

Our Portfolio

Our simulated portfolio comprises a diversified range of assets, including equities, fixed income, and alternative investments. We actively manage our portfolio, adapting to changing market conditions and seeking opportunities to maximize risk-adjusted returns. Through regular portfolio reviews and performance evaluations, we strive to continuously improve our investment strategies and enhance our overall performance.

Learning and Development

Veritage Capital serves as a platform for students to develop essential skills and knowledge in the field of finance. Our members gain practical experience in portfolio management, financial analysis, and investment research. We also have a dedicated reports page on our website that showcases a selection of our equity research reports, providing valuable insights into our investment process and decision-making.

Additionally, we hold weekly meetings to discuss market trends, investment opportunities, and other relevant topics, fostering a collaborative learning environment for our members.
