About Us

Veritage Capital is a student-run, simulated investment fund dedicated to providing hands-on experience in portfolio management and financial analysis. Our members conduct in-depth research across diverse asset classes, utilising both fundamental and technical analysis to inform investment decisions.

We hold regular meetings to discuss market trends and potential strategies, refining our approach through continuous portfolio review and performance evaluation. Our website features a selection of our research reports, demonstrating our commitment to learning and professional development.

Join Us

If you are a passionate and ambitious student with a keen interest in finance, we invite you to join Veritage Capital. Whether you aspire to a career in investment banking, asset management, or any other field within finance, Veritage Capital provides a unique opportunity to gain practical experience, build valuable skills, and network with industry professionals.

To apply, simply fill out our Google form. We recruit on a rolling basis and aim to get back to you within two weeks of your application.
