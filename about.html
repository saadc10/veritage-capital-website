About Us

Veritage Capital is a student-run investment fund providing hands-on portfolio management experience to aspiring finance professionals. Our research-driven approach focuses on identifying and analysing potential investment opportunities across diverse asset classes using a combination of fundamental and technical analysis.

We maintain a diversified portfolio and hold weekly meetings to discuss market trends and investment ideas. Through regular portfolio reviews and performance evaluations, we continuously refine our strategies and aim to maximise risk-adjusted returns. Our dedicated reports page showcases a selection of our equity research.
